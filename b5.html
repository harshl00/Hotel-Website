Hotel Details

Featuring free WiFi and a restaurant, Hotel Uddhav Vilas offers accommodation in Udaipur. Free private parking is available on site. The rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Each room has a private bathroom and air-conditioning. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. City Palace of Udaipur is 1.4 km from Hotel Uddhav Vilas, while Jagdish temple is 1.4 km away. The nearest airport is Maharana Pratap Airport, 20 km from the property. Udaipur Railway Station is 1.4 km.
