Hotel Details

KUMBHAL PALACE RESORT Enjoy a Luxury Experience The Kumbhal Palace Resort is a popular hotel located in Kumbhalgarh, Rajasthan, India. It is known for its beautiful surroundings and proximity to the historic Kumbhalgarh Fort, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Kumbhal Palace Resort is situated in the Aravalli hills, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding natural beauty. It is approximately 2 kilometers away from the Kumbhalgarh Fort. The resort features well-appointed rooms and suites, designed with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay for guests. Each room is elegantly furnished and offers a serene ambiance. The resort offers a range of facilities to enhance guests' experience. This includes a multi-cuisine restaurant, swimming pool and indoor/outdoor recreational activities. The staff at the resort is known for their hospitality and goes above and beyond to cater to guests' needs.
